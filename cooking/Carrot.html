CARROT HALWA KERAL STYLE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1kg Carrot

  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoon Whole wheat flour (called chapati flour or Atta)

  • 1 pinch Saffron (or 1/2 tsp of food colour )

  • 5 tablespoon Corn Flour

  • Pistachios, cashews,almonds & raisins as required

  • 4-5 Cardamom crushed or powder

  • 1 bowl Sugar(250g)use the same bowl to measure 1/2 bowl of water.
