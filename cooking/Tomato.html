TOMATO RICE

Ingredients

  • 2 bowls cooked rice

  • 2 sliced tomatoes

  • 1 sliced onion

  • 1 tomato to decorate

  • Salt to the taste

  • 1/2 tbsp cumin seeds (jeera)

  • 2-3 green chillies

  • Coriander

  • 1 tbsp coriander & cumin powder (Dhaniya-jeera powder)

  • 1 tbsp red chilli powder

  • 2-3 tbsp cooking oil
